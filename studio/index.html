AI dashboard

Model confidence

How certain the AI is about its current scene prediction. Below 17% means untrained; 50%+ is decent; 70%+ is well-trained. —

Scene training

Each bar shows the AI's confidence for that scene. Click a scene to pin it as the training target — the model continuously learns from it while pinned. Click again to unpin.

Visualization

Type

The visual style used to represent audio. Each style has a different look and feel.

Quality

Detail level of the audio analysis. Higher values are more accurate but heavier on the CPU. Acceptable range: 5–15.

Smoothing

How smoothly values transition between frames. 0 means no smoothing; 1 is maximum smoothness.

Focus

The center volume level the analysis focuses on. Adjust to emphasize specific loudness ranges.

Spread

The volume range around the focus point. Wider means more dynamic range is captured.
